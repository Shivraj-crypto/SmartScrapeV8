##  About Sugarfina

In 2012, founders Rosie O'Neil and Josh Resnick brought their idea of a sophisticated candy destination for adults to life through Sugarfina. Inspired by the movie Willy Wonka & the Chocolate Factory, Sugarfina sells champagne-infused gummy bears, artisanal chocolate treats, and more sourced from all over the world. Sugarfina also lets you personalize candy gifts with its signature Candy Bento Boxes, which are perfect for gifting.
