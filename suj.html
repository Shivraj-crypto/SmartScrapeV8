Sugarfina Store Info

What are the best ways to save more at Sugarfina?

These sweet savings tips can be applied on Sugarfina's website, so shopping there doesn't leave a sour taste in your mouth.

Subscribe to Sugarfina's Email List

Are you a first-time buyer on Sugarfina.com? You can get 15% off your very first purchase. All you need to do is subscribe to Sugarfina's email newsletter by entering your email address in the pop-up you'll see displayed on your screen when you visit the site. The newsletter will also give you insider information about upcoming discounts and deals so you can prepare to take advantage of them.

Utilize the Free Shipping Discount

You can ship your order for free on Sugarfina by simply making purchases above $150. This shouldn't be hard to achieve because this shop stocks luxury confectionery priced way above what you'd normally pay for everyday candies.

Take Advantage of Free Gifts

You can receive free gifts from Sugarfina when you make orders worth over several amounts, including $100, $135, and $160. These gifts include chocolate bars, candy cubes, a 3-piece bento box, and more. The gift you will receive with your order will depend on the code you use, and you can only use one code per order. Check the website for these codes because they change over time.

Pay Later at Zero Interest Fees

Craving a confectionery but can't afford it right now? You can still order it from Sugarfina, then pay your dues later. The best part? You'll pay the price that appears on the website with no added costs (read no interest). To utilize this offer, select AfterPay during checkout as your payment method.

About Sugarfina

In 2012, founders Rosie O'Neil and Josh Resnick brought their idea of a sophisticated candy destination for adults to life through Sugarfina. Inspired by the movie Willy Wonka & the Chocolate Factory, Sugarfina sells champagne-infused gummy bears, artisanal chocolate treats, and more sourced from all over the world. Sugarfina also lets you personalize candy gifts with its signature Candy Bento Boxes, which are perfect for gifting.

What is Sugarfina's shipping policy?

Sugafina offers three shipping options: standard, expedited, and express. If you use standard shipping, your order will reach you in five to seven days, while expedited shipping will take two to three business days. Express shipping will take between one and two business days. Worth noting is that these times don't factor in processing time, which can vary between several hours and five business days.

Can I return candy bought from Sugarfina.com?

No, Sugarfina does not allow customers to make returns due to the perishable nature of candy. If you purchase products from Sugarfina's authorized partners, you can refer to its return policy on your receipt for exceptions (if any).

Can I combine several coupon codes at checkout?

No, Sugarfina only lets you use one coupon code for every order you make on its website.

Does Sugarfina have physical stores?
